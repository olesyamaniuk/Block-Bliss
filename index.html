Game Description

Block Bliss: Block Puzzle is a classic block game with a modern twist. Arrange colorful blocks on a 10x10 grid to clear lines and score points. With no time constraints, you can strategize each move at your own pace. It’s the perfect blend of relaxation and brain-training, suitable for casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike. Complete daily challenges, compete on global leaderboards, and customize your gameplay experience with a variety of themes. Dive into a world of fun, strategy, and relaxation with Block Bliss!
